GETTING STARTED

Hello! I'm Kristine Mae Montajas, I’m a passionate individual who loves to explore new ideas and challenge myself creatively. Ever since I was young, I’ve had a deep curiosity for how things work, especially when it comes to technology. I find joy in solving problems and building things from scratch. My journey has taken me through different paths, but each step helped me grow and learn more about myself. I believe in lifelong learning and constantly strive to improve both professionally and personally.

Way back from 2023 to 2024, I worked as a sales clerk at Urban and a cashier at a Chinese store. At Urban, I assisted customers with their purchases, organized shelves, and helped maintain a clean, welcoming store environment. I also provided product recommendations and contributed to meeting sales targets. Though my main role wasn’t cashiering, I occasionally helped operate the POS during busy hours. Meanwhile, at the Chinese store, I was fully in charge of the cashier duties handling payments, giving correct change, and balancing the register. I also supported the team by bagging items, stocking products, and keeping the counter area clean. Working in both fast-paced settings helped me become more responsible, detail-oriented, and customer-focused. I learned to communicate clearly, work under pressure, and adapt to different kinds of customers. These experiences greatly improved my confidence and prepared me for future roles in retail or customer service.
